The Problem

Agricultural markets in Kenya lack transparent, real-time pricing information, leading to inefficient trading and unfair pricing for farmers and consumers.

Information Asymmetry

Farmers lack access to current market prices, leading to exploitation by middlemen and unfair pricing.

Delayed Price Discovery

Price information takes days to reach rural areas, causing market inefficiencies and lost opportunities.

Market Fragmentation

Disconnected regional markets prevent optimal price formation and efficient resource allocation.

Our Solution

A comprehensive real-time market prices dashboard that provides transparent, accurate, and accessible commodity pricing information.

Real-time Price Updates

Live market data updated every 30 seconds during trading hours, ensuring users have the most current pricing information.

Advanced Analytics

Comprehensive market analysis including price volatility, moving averages, and market efficiency indicators.

Mobile-First Design

Responsive interface optimized for mobile devices, ensuring accessibility for farmers and traders in rural areas.
